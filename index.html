Why This Book?

Many years ago, as a young adult I noticed in the life of married men and women things that gave me pain and burden. I saw lots of men and women who were going through unbearable situations in their marriages. I saw also some men who sees their wives as trash in their eyes and their wives valueless. Some prefer other women to their wives.
I saw lot of men and women who thought that devoice and separation is the best for them. ‘Behind the woman’ is a book born out of conviction that a man should go extra miles to restore and maintain peace and harmony in His home. I am of the opinion that victory is in our hands and that is in fact based on our reactions and attitudes to your marital challenges.
Men play your part in that home and Almighty God will grant you victory, no matter how difficult and stubborn your wife is, God will restore peace in your home One can chase a thousand but two will chase ten thousand…. Deut 32:30

Meet The Author

Evang. Chinwe C. Okpara is an associate minister of Great Light of God Ministry Germany, also a minister with Mountain of fire and Miracles Ministry. A minister at African Christian fellowship. (ACF) She is the founder and president of young minister’s wife forum.

Evang. Chinwe C. Okpara

Author

She was mentored by Pastor Chris Okpara, The G.O Great Light of God Ministry Germany.
She was a head teacher of leaders Ville school, Lambe for many years. She is a home builder and marriage counselor, a teacher by profession and a teacher of the word. She has the zeal in her to make sure that peace reign in marriages even the most turbulent ones. She believes there is no bad marriage. Her message is that “GOD MUST RESTORE BACK HOMES” She published some articles in some magazines. A graduate of university of Uyo. She has BA. ED. She has a happy family. They are blessed with children, Victor and Testimony.
